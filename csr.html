CSR

Sustainability

Pan Rama recognizes it's responsibility in preserving our environment, and commits to do its part to ensure sustainable practices across all our operations. We have well defined and measurable policies and practices including:

ENERGY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

  • Maximum LED lighting
  • Efficient lighting, cooling & ventilation systems
  • Progressive use of energy-saving servo motors in sewing machines

WASTE MANAGEMENT

  • Segregation of waste
  • Routing paper, plastic & fabric waste to verified recycling facilities
  • Chemical waste – separate containment area

GOING GREEN

  • “Save our Trees” drive, going paperles, printing only when necessary
  • Recycle & reuse paper
  • Encouraging use of re-usable containers, phasing out single-use plastic

Community

Giving back to our employees and the community
