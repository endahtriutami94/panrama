About Us

Established since 1984 in Jakarta, PT. Pan Rama is engaged in the manufacturing & export of Ladies Fashion Apparel to the United States, Europe and Australia. In 1998, we were the pioneers in relocating our production base to Central Java, which has now become a strategic manufacturing hub. Located in Solo, often referred to as the home of Batik, we avail of it’s competent labor pool and close proximity to shipping port of Semarang.

Our Factories

With two production facilities, Pan Rama has close to 40 production lines, with a monthly capacity of 350,000 units. We have continued to specialize in the ladies fashion space, with particular emphasis in the Soft Dressing categories. Having developed our niche in this market, Pan Rama has cultivated the skills and expertise in the better Dress & Blouse segment. We work closely together with brand name partners in the development and production of their season collections, and take pride in being considered the “go-to” factory in Indonesia for fine Ladies Fashionwear.
