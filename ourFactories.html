With two production facilities in the Central Java region, Pan Rama has close to 40 production lines, with a monthly capacity of 350,000 units. We have continued to specialize in the ladies fashion space, with particular emphasis in the Soft Dressing categories. Having developed our niche in this market, Pan Rama has cultivated the skills and expertise in the better Dress & Blouse segment. We work closely together with brand name partners in the development and production of their season collections, and take pride in being considered the “go-to” factory in Indonesia for fine Ladies Fashionwear.
